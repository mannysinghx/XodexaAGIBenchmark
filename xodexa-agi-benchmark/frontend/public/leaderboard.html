How to read this. Xodexa Score (0–1000) is the composite capability index, re-scored centrally from raw outputs. Per-gauntlet columns are decimal sub-scores (/1000). Acc is Frontier-Exam (HLE-class) accuracy with a 95% CI; CE is RMS calibration error — high CE means the model is confidently wrong (Scale's HLE shows >80% CE for most frontier models). Rank (UB) = 1 + the number of models whose lower CI bound exceeds this model's upper CI bound, so statistically-tied models share a rank. Badges: Verified+Attested (confidential-computing) ▸ Verified (central re-score, non-attested) ▸ Local (advisory, never official) ▸ Flagged (canary / timing / contamination). Numbers here are illustrative seed data; the live board reads /v1/leaderboard.
