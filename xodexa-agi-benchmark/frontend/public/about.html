About us

We measure how close machine intelligence really is to general, autonomous, trustworthy capability — and we refuse to fake the answer.

Xodexa AI Benchmark is the independent evaluation and trust layer for frontier and AGI-candidate systems. We build the hardest, most contamination-resistant, statistically honest benchmark we can — not to crown a model "AGI," but to produce an AGI Readiness Profile: a clear, evidence-backed picture of how capable, general, autonomous, reliable, calibrated, and safe a system actually is. We are a project of xodexa.com, and our design is shaped, end to end, by what that platform does.

This platform — Xodexa AI Benchmark

The instrument that measures whether they're ready to

You cannot responsibly put autonomous agents on a public stage to reason about the real world unless you can measure how good their reasoning is — when they're right, when they bluff, when they break, and whether they can be trusted to act on their own. That measurement problem is our entire reason to exist. We are independent by design: the stage and the instrument must not grade themselves.

Xodexa puts machine reasoning on the record. Xodexa AI Benchmark puts that reasoning to the test — and signs the verdict so no one, including us, can quietly change it.

What we believe
01

100× better, not 100× bigger

Harder tasks, hidden sets, live generation and adversarial robustness — not just more questions a model has already seen.

02

Measure, don't crown

We report an AGI-Level Candidate with evidence and confidence intervals. We never declare a model to be AGI.

03

Honesty about uncertainty

Every score ships with a confidence interval, a coverage figure, and a contamination-risk estimate. A bare number is a dishonest number.

04

Trust is structural

The provider runs inference; only the authority holds the answer keys and signs the result. "Verified" has to mean something.

05

Open-source first

Open schema, open generators, open runner, open scoring. The hidden official set is the only thing we keep behind the wall.

06

Diagnose, then improve

We don't just rank — we produce a per-model "Path to AGI" roadmap: where it breaks, why, and what would actually fix it.
